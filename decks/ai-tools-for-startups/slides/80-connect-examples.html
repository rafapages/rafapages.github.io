Things you can automate this week

Lead magnet

Free asset, captured email

Someone wants your checklist or template. They fill a form, get the file instantly — and you get their email on your list. No AI required, just plumbing.

Form filled → Deliver file → Save to list
Newsletter

Newsletter that writes itself

On a schedule, AI pulls your latest posts, links and notes, drafts the issue in your voice, and queues it for one-click review before it ships.

Source ideas → AI drafts → Queue to send
Daily digest

Your news, summarised by 8am

Each morning it scans the sources you care about, AI boils them down to the five things that matter, and drops a tidy brief in your inbox.

Collect sources → AI summarises → Email you
Daily planner

Your day, planned for you

It pulls your open tasks from Jira, your inbox and your notes, then AI builds a prioritised action plan — what to tackle first, what can wait.

Gather tasks → AI prioritises → Action plan
Support

Inbox triage on autopilot

A support email arrives. AI reads it, classifies the topic, drafts a reply and routes urgent ones to a human — so nothing sits unanswered overnight.

New ticket → AI sort + draft → Reply / route
Meetings

Notes & tasks, done for you

A call wraps up. AI turns the transcript into a clean recap, pulls out the action items, emails everyone and creates the to-dos automatically.

Call ends → AI recap + tasks → Send + create
