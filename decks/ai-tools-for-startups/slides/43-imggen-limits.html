So here's why it does weird things

Hands, text & counting

It matches the texture of a scene, not rules like "5 fingers" or correct spelling on a sign. Garbled text and extra fingers are the classic tell.

Hard to control or repeat

You're steering a cloud, not editing a canvas. Reproducing an exact thing, or changing one detail while keeping the rest, is genuinely difficult.

Bias & sameness

"CEO," "beautiful," "doctor" reflect training-data patterns. Outputs drift toward an averaged, often stereotyped, house aesthetic.

Copyright & provenance

Trained on scraped images, including copyrighted and artists' work. Real legal and brand-safety questions for anything commercial — know your tool's licensing.

!

No real-world facts

It has no notion of truth — only what looks right. It will happily render a plausible-looking but fake product, logo, or "photo" of an event.
