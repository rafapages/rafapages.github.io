Video is the newest frontier — handle with care

A text prompt can now produce broadcast-looking footage. It's spectacular in a demo but hard to control in real work, so know what it's good for before committing on a deadline.

+ Where it pays today
  • Social & ad b-roll — short, atmospheric clips you'd otherwise license from stock.
  • Pitch & sizzle reels — a striking few seconds to open a deck or demo day.
  • Concept & mood films — show an idea before you've built or shot anything.
– Where it still bites
  • Precise control — getting an exact action, product, or logo right is fiddly and slow.
  • Continuity — same character or scene across shots drifts between generations.
  • Time & cost — many takes to get one usable second; easy to burn an afternoon.
