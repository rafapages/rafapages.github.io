The one-sentence version

At its core an LLM is sophisticated autocomplete — reasoning and search are how we make it reliable.

  • The base model is plausible, not correct: great for drafts, summaries, brainstorming, code starters.
  • Reasoning buys you logic and multi-step accuracy; search buys you real, current, and proprietary facts. The most useful products combine both.
  • The companies winning aren't the ones with the best model — they're the ones who designed around the limits with the right scaffolding.
