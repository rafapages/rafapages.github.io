AI Tools for Startups
03

Campaign creator

From a one-line brief to a full first draft.

I
Instructions

From a brief, give a tagline, three channel variants, and a calendar. Warm, plain-spoken, never salesy.

K
Knowledge

Brand guidelines, tone-of-voice doc, campaigns that worked, the product catalogue.

A
Actions

Search the web for trends, write copy, build a calendar in a spreadsheet.

Result

“Launch the summer collection, budget-conscious audience, three weeks” → an on-brand, multi-channel campaign draft in minutes instead of a blank-page afternoon.
