Build · The workflow

Don't start by coding. Start by spec-ing

The mistake is to throw "build me an app" at the AI and hope. The pros do one step first: they write down exactly what they want, then hand that to the builder.

1

Idea

A real problem you have, like "a tracker for my customer interviews."

2

Spec

Write the what with Claude: the pages, data and behaviour, as a markdown doc or HTML mockup. This is the work.

3

Build

Hand the spec to Claude Code or Lovable. It scaffolds the whole thing from your doc.

4

Iterate

Try it, point at what's wrong, update the spec, regenerate. Repeat until it's right.

Why it works: a vague prompt gives vague software. A clear spec is the difference between a toy and a tool, and it keeps the AI on the rails as the project grows.
