Crafting your prompt: seven dials

These are the modifiers that turn a generic result into a deliberate one. Borrow the vocabulary of photography and film — the model was trained on millions of captioned images that used exactly these words.

1

Cameras & Lenses

Canon, Nikon, Polaroid, GoPro — each carries a built-in look.

2

Angles & Shots

Low, high, side, bird's-eye — the camera's point of view.

3

Lighting

Sunlight, studio, lamp, reflection — mood lives here.

4

Filmmakers

"In the style of…" borrows a whole visual signature.

5

Photographers

Names map to recognisable framing, grain and palette.

6

Themes

Colour stories and aesthetics that tie a set together.

7

Material

Clay, glass, metal, paper — what the thing is "made of."

Pick a few, stack them, and the result stops being generic and starts being yours.
