AI Tools for Startups
02

CV evaluator

The same yardstick applied to every single candidate.

I
Instructions

Score 1–10 on five criteria. Justify each score. Never infer age or gender. Output a table.

K
Knowledge

The job description, your scoring rubric, a few “strong” and “weak” examples to calibrate.

A
Actions

Read PDF / Word files, output a comparison table.

Result

Drop in 30 CVs → a ranked table with the same rubric on every one. No drift between the first you read fresh and the thirtieth you read tired. Consistency is the thing a one-off chat can't give you.
