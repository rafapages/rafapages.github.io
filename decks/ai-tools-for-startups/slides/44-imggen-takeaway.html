The one-sentence version

LLMs predict the next word. Diffusion models remove noise, step by step — and both give you the plausible, not the correct.

  • Use them where plausible and fast wins: concepts, mood boards, drafts, marketing variations, placeholder art.
  • Add human review and guardrails for anything customer-facing — especially around brand accuracy, bias, and licensing.
  • Same lesson as before: the winners aren't those with the fanciest model, but those who design around what it can't do.
